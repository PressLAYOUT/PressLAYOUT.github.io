About PRESSLAYOUT

We ar Preparing impositions of single file pages into plate ready files for a variety of printing presses while adhering to job ticket specifications, Standard Operating Procedures, and Detailed Work Instructions. PressLayout provides a prepress imposition, or the production of printing forms in pdf format. All you have to do is send the basic information about the product and prepress in PDF format, and we will return the digitally imposed pdf, made in the Kodak Preps application, to you as soon as possible. All you have to do is insert that PDF into your RIP, or into the further process of your production.
